About

Infrastructure fails at small, high-stress regions long before visible damage appears. InfraHeal embeds response into the material itself.

Why it matters

Localized damage drives most structural failure. Reactive repairs increase cost, downtime, and risk—especially under climate stress.

What we deliver

A composite platform that senses stress concentration and activates healing where it matters—improving durability without “heal everywhere” waste.

How we build trust

Experiment-first development with clear feasibility milestones, validated using standard materials characterization and mechanical testing.

Technology

Stress localization + targeted actuation. A practical path from lab validation to deployable infrastructure materials.

Stress-selective self-healing composites

InfraHeal’s platform integrates shape-memory polymer (SMP) actuation with fiber-reinforced mechanics to enable controlled crack closure at high-risk zones. The result is efficient use of healing capacity and improved service life.

  • Stress localization through fiber-enabled response
  • Localized activation (thermal or magnetic) for targeted healing
  • Feasibility validated at coupon-level in Phase I scope
